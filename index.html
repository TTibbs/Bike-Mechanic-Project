Safe Wheels is a family-run bike mechanic business in London, ENG.
We provide expert repair and maintenance services for all types of bikes.
Our staff are experienced and friendly, and we strive to provide quality
work at competitive prices. Come see us today for all your cycling needs!

About Us

We are passionate about helping people get the most out of their bicycles.
From repairs and maintenance to custom builds and complete bike overhauls,
we make sure everyone is equipped with the best bikes and riding experience.
Our mechanics are all experienced and knowledgeable and are
always eager to help our customers find the best solutions for their bike needs.
We offer a wide range of services to ensure our customers are satisfied with their bicycles.
From tune-ups and repairs to upgrades and custom builds, we are dedicated to making
sure every customer leaves with the bike of their dreams. We also offer a variety of
accessories and parts to help make sure everyone is prepared for the ride.
Our team of mechanics is always available to answer any questions and provide the best service possible.
